What is Scrum?

SCRUM...

  • ... is a framework that challenges the traditional, sequential approach of project management.
  • ... is a framework for effective team collaboration around complex adaptive problems.
  • ... is used for developing, delivering and sustaining complex products, mainly software development.
  • ... focus to deliver business value.
  • ... makes it easy for people to work towards a clear goal in an agile process.
  • ... break down the main goal into smaller goals, called sprints, where they stop for evaluation during the journey.
  • ... involves daily interaction within the team.
  • ... has sprints that lasts normally two to four weeks.
  • ... uses a continously updated and changed specification of requirements, called a backlog.
  • ... has three specified roles; Scrum master, Product owner and the Team.
